# Google-Store-Analytics-Project
Google Merchandise Store analysis using Google Analytics 4, focusing on customer behaviour, acquisition channels, e-commerce performance, funnel analysis and strategic marketing recommendations.


## Google Merchandise Store Analysis

### Overview

This project analyses the performance of the Google Merchandise Store using Google Analytics 4 data. The objective was to evaluate user behaviour, traffic acquisition, customer engagement and e-commerce performance, translating analytical findings into actionable business recommendations.

Rather than focusing solely on reporting metrics, the project connects digital analytics with marketing strategy by identifying opportunities to improve customer acquisition, conversion, engagement and retention.

---

### Objectives

* Analyse website traffic and user behaviour
* Evaluate acquisition channels and engagement quality
* Identify customer segments and personas
* Analyse navigation paths and the e-commerce funnel
* Evaluate product and category performance
* Develop strategic recommendations supported by data

---

### Tools

---

### Key Areas of Analysis

* Strategic business recommendations

---

### Key Insights

Some of the main findings include:

* Returning users represented the highest-value customer segment despite accounting for a relatively small proportion of total visitors.
* Mobile users generated substantial traffic but experienced significantly lower engagement and conversion than desktop users.
* The largest conversion loss occurred between product views and add-to-cart events.
* Several product categories attracted high levels of interest but underperformed in final conversions, highlighting opportunities for UX and merchandising improvements.
* Data quality issues within GA4 tracking suggested opportunities to improve tagging and analytics implementation.

---

### Outcome

The project concludes with prioritised business recommendations covering:

* Customer acquisition
* Conversion optimisation
* Mobile experience
* Customer retention
* Data quality
* Marketing strategy

The final deliverable is a complete digital analytics report combining quantitative analysis with business-focused recommendations.
